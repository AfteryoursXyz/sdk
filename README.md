# Afteryours

**Afteryours** is a decentralized protocol that acts as your digital executor.  
Set the rules today. Let smart contracts and AI ensure your legacy, assets, and final messages are executed exactly as you intended.

## 📌 About Afteryours

Afteryours enables anyone to:

- Define how digital assets should be distributed
- Store and schedule final messages
- Assign beneficiaries with verifiable conditions
- Automate execution through smart contracts
- Leverage AI for validation, orchestration, and instruction management

All done in a **trustless, transparent, and intermediary-free** environment.

---

## 🎯 Vision

To build a global standard for blockchain-based digital inheritance management, where:

- Users retain full control  
- Execution is automatic and secure  
- Privacy is preserved  
- No centralized institution is required  

---

## ⚙️ How It Works

1. **Set the Rules**  
   Define asset distribution logic, beneficiaries, and message conditions.

2. **Lock with Smart Contracts**  
   Your rules are encoded and secured on-chain via immutable smart contracts.

3. **AI Validation Layer**  
   AI assists in validating predefined conditions and orchestrating execution flows.

4. **Automated Execution**  
   Once conditions are met, assets are distributed and messages are delivered automatically.
